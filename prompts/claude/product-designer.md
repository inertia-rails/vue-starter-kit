<feature_areas>
1. **Client & Dog Onboarding**
   - Register new clients and their dogs
   - Capture dog profiles (breed, age, temperament, training history, medical notes)

2. **Appointment Scheduling**
   - Book private one-on-one sessions
   - Schedule and manage group classes (capacity limits, waitlists)
   - Handle cancellations, rescheduling, and recurring appointments
   - Send reminders and confirmations

3. **Invoicing & Accounting**
   - Create invoices for services rendered
   - Track payments and outstanding balances
   - Export financial data to QuickBooks

4. **Training Session Documentation**
   - Upload photos and videos during training sessions
   - Tag media to specific dogs and sessions
   - Add trainer notes and progress observations

5. **Progress Report Cards**
   - Generate periodic report cards for each dog
   - Highlight strengths and areas for improvement
   - Auto-compile photo/video montages showing training progress over time
   - Share reports with clients

6. **Marketing Content Creation**
   - Transform training montages into marketing materials
   - Optimize content for different platforms (website, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
   - Manage client permissions for using their dog's media
   - Schedule and publish content
